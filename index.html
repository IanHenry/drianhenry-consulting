Dr Ian Henry

1. Overview

I provide strategic consulting and advisory services for organisations in life sciences, healthcare, and data-driven industries — helping them design, implement, and scale effective data and digital strategies.

With experience spanning academia, biotech, and corporate R&D — including leadership of data science strategy at AstraZeneca and advisory roles across European research institutes — I bridge the gap between technical innovation and organisational impact.

Consulting services help organisations: - Define and deliver clear data strategies aligned with business goals
- Implement FAIR and reproducible data governance frameworks
- Build capacity in AI, data science, and digital transformation
- Translate complex data into actionable insight for science and strategy
- Develop teams and culture for sustainable, data-driven decision-making
